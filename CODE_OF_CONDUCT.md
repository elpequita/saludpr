# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

SaludPR is a public-good project serving the people of Puerto Rico. We pledge to make participation a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, experience level, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Standards

Examples of positive behavior:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language (English and Spanish both welcomed)
- Respecting differing viewpoints and experiences
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the community and the mission

Unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Using this project's data or tools to harm communities we aim to serve
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate

## Enforcement

Violations may be reported to: carlos.perez@dataurea.com

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
